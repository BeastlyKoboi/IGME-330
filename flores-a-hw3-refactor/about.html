About My App

Welcome to my Audio Visualizer App

This audio vizualizer is an interesting way of engaging with audio files. At present it only has 3 files to choose from, but I intend to expand that capability in the future. To use it, press play. You can use the controls below the canvas to control the colors of the sprites and several audio/visual effects.
